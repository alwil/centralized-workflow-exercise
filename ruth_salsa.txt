Ingredients for salsa:

* 12 tomatoes
* one onion sliced in rings
* 2 jalapenos
* 2 limes

Instructions

1) Blend the tomatoes
2) Fry the onions in a big pan on low with olive oil
3) add the tomatoes to the pan and slowly simmer them for 25 minutes
4) squeeze in the juice of 2 limes

Enjoy over tacos with cheese and steak!
